Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this rafting site is to establish an online presence for our rafting business, providing information about our services, trips, and safety measures, and facilitating bookings and inquiries from potential customers.

Audience

Our target audience includes outdoor enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and families looking for fun activities. We aim to attract individuals aged 18-45 with a moderate to high disposable income who are interested in recreational outdoor activities.

Scenario Questions:

  • Q: Are there any family-friendly rafting trips? A: Yes, we offer trips suitable for families with children.
  • Q: What safety measures are in place? A: We provide all safety gear and have certified guides on all trips.
  • Q: How can I book a trip? A: You can book a trip through our website or by contacting us directly.
